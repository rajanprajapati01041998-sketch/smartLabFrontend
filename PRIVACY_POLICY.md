## 2. Information we collect
### A) Account & authentication information
When you log in, the App may process information required for authentication (for example, credentials and session-related data). The App stores an authentication token and user information on your device for persistence.

### B) Patient and laboratory related information
As part of healthcare/laboratory workflows, the App may display, collect, and transmit patient-related data and related administrative details needed to provide services (e.g., registrations, sample/test details, reports, reprints, refunds, and settlements).

### C) Device information
The App may collect basic device information (e.g., device details) to support functionality and reliability.

### D) Location information (optional / role-based)
If enabled, the App uses device location services:
- The App requests location permission and may obtain **latitude and longitude**.
- The App converts coordinates to an address (approximate address lookup) and uses these details to support live tracking and operational needs.
- The App may receive real-time location updates (e.g., through server-sent events) related to field/off-site staff.

### E) Network & IP information
The App may obtain the device’s local IP address.

### F) Payment-related information
If you use in-app payment features, payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers according to their own privacy policies.

## 3. How we use information
We use collected information to:
- Provide access to the App and authenticate users.
- Support healthcare/laboratory operations and workflows.
- Display and print receipts/reports and manage reprints.
- Manage refunds/settlements and related records.
- Enable live tracking/location features (where permitted and enabled).
- Maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and improve performance.

## 4. How we share information
We may share information:
- With the App’s backend services (our servers) to perform healthcare operations and deliver real-time updates.
- With payment processors (if applicable) for payment handling.
- With third parties only where required to operate the App, comply with legal obligations, or protect rights/safety.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 5. Data retention
We retain information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and local regulations.

## 6. Your choices and controls
- **Location permission:** You can revoke location permission in your device settings. If you do so, location-based features may stop working.
- **Logout:** You can log out of the App, which may clear locally stored authentication data.

## 7. Security
We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Children’s privacy
This App is intended for business/healthcare use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us.

## 9. International data transfers
If you access the App from outside the country where we operate, information may be transferred and processed in accordance with applicable laws.
